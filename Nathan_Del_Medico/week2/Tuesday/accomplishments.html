Accomplishments

Larry Wall is the author of the rn Usernet Client and the widely used patch program. He has won the International Obfuscated C Code Contest twice and was the recipient of the first Free Software Foundation Award for the Advancement of Free Software in 1998.
Wall developed the Perl interpreter and language while working for System Development Corporation, which later became part of Unisys.

He is the co-author of Programming Perl, which is the definitive resource for Perl programmers; and edited the Perl Cookbook. He then became employed full-time by O'Reilly Media to further develop Perl and write books on the subject.
